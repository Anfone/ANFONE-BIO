"Senior Year"



Senior high school is the best recollection I have of being a student.
This was the point at which you discovered that life was full of mysteries.
Because at this point in my life, mystery is a phrase of experience.
This period taught me that life isn't just about happiness or sadness,
but that these two will always be a part of me and my closest buddy in life.
I've had a lot of experiences that have shaped my personality.
That I may treasure throughout my life's journey.

We all remember our senior year of high school as a time
in our lives filled with a variety of feelings such as tension,
worry, excitement, and happiness. Seniors in high school have a wide range of feelings and emotions.
That last year marks the end of a period in life, the transition from being
a teenager to leaving behind the life of a high schooler. That is why having unique experiences
and making lifelong memories is so crucial at this age.
