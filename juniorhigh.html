"Junior Year"



I'm attend in Gun-ob High School   As you can see, your school days will be busy;
but they will also be filled with new experiences, challenges, and fun!
As a junior high school, the typical day of class can seem to run very, very long.
however, but to get a full education one needs a combination of them.
I can assure you that every high schooler attends class for a similar amount of time.
High school shapes you into the person you are now.

I have wonderful memories,both good and bad, some of which were educational
and some of which I will cherish for the rest of my life.
My high school experience influenced my development as a person both inside
and outside of the classroom by making me more independent, strategically selecting friends,
and teachers encouraging me to go college and achieve goals I set for myself.
