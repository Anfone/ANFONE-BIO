"Elementary Year"


 

Hello i'm Rosalie Layaguin Anfone and I was born on June 5, 2003
in Lorega Cebu City
I was raise in Lapu-Lapu City (Home Care Center)
so many things happened during my years in elementary school.
And yet, so much of it has been blurred
in my mind or erased completely from my memory.
However, i'll never forget my first day of school
in elementary at lapu-lapu city central
i remember being excited ,because i will meet a new friend in the class.
As a student, I wasn't always the brightest,
and I don't think I want to be either.
When I was in elementary school, I wanted to know everything there was
to know about every subject my teachers had taught me.
I believe I did well since I never experienced any class disruptions.
When I had less than a year left in my six years of elementary school.
